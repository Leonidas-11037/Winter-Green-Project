Conclusion

I believe this landscape of Wintergreen is well maintained due to the cut down remains staying in the area to be used by the ecosystem, thus having the process of self-renewal occur naturally. For an area near a collage residence hall, it is important to maintain the health of this area and not to tamper with it too much. It showcases outcome of preserving land that went maintained by efforts to keep the land healthy. It’s layered and has a long way down. It has variety in the scenes to be captured. Overall, the view from Wintergreen Gorge is beautiful with the sense of height and the layered land. It’s near a residence hall that many call home, so it must be respected like how one tends to a backyard garden, except significantly more dangerous with the lack of railings.
